The Grove

Stillgrove is not just a location. It is a memory that never fully settled. Trees stand where they always have, but something about them feels delayed. Light lingers longer than it should. Footsteps echo even after the walker has disappeared.
You may notice a branch moving — and then moving again, slightly differently. A figure crossing the same path twice, but not quite the same way. A sound that arrives before the action that caused it.
Everything here happens in layers of time. We’re not gonna spell it out for you — you’ll need to watch carefully and piece things together yourself. The deeper you go, the more you begin to understand: time here is not broken. It is layered.
