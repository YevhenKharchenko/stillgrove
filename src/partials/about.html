About the Game

A Runner That Keeps Growing

Long Neck Run takes a familiar endless-run style idea and gives it a playful twist. Instead of simply dodging and sprinting forward, you build your character as you go. Every correctly collected ring adds height, changes how you move through the level, and brings you closer to a bigger finish-line reward.

The whole thing’s pretty ridiculous and colorful — which honestly, that’s kinda the point. It’s not trying to be some deep, meaningful experience — just stay alert, react fast, and enjoy the weirdly satisfying feeling of getting ridiculously tall while everything tries to knock you down.
