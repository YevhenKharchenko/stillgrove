The Echo Mechanic

Nothing Happens Only Once

Every action leaves a trace. In Stillgrove, those traces don’t disappear — they remain, quietly repeating themselves in the background.
These echoes are not decorations. They are part of the puzzle.
You might see your past self walking across a clearing. You might watch an object fall again and again, each time slightly out of sync. These repetitions are clues, tools, and sometimes obstacles.

Figuring out when these echoes show up is crucial. You’ll need to watch for patterns — when they appear, how they overlap, what happens when different versions of the same moment crash into each other.
This is not about reacting quickly. It is about noticing patterns that others would ignore.
