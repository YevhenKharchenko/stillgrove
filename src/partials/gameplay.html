How to Play

Simple Controls, Fast Decisions

  • Run Forward

    Guide your character along the track with smooth finger controls.

  • Collect Matching Rings

    Pick up rings of the correct color to grow your neck higher.

  • Avoid the Wrong Ones

    Wrong-color rings reduce your height and make the run harder.

  • Dodge Obstacles

    Stay alert as barriers, tricky paths, and hazards appear ahead.

  • Reach the Finish

    Cross the line with as much height to earn better rewards.

  • Short Note

    It looks easy at first. Then the track starts asking real questions.
