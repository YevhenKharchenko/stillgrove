The Core Fun

Height Is Progress

Look, I know it sounds dumb, but there’s something oddly addictive about watching your character grow taller. Can’t put my finger on why exactly, but it just works. Every ring gives immediate feedback, every mistake is visible, and every finish becomes a small payoff for how well you handled the run.
That visual progress keeps the game lively. You are not just moving forward — you are building a result in real time. A short level can turn dramatic very quickly when you are one clean move away from a huge finish or one mistake away from losing your advantage.
